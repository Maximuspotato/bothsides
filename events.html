Session format Past Events

• Introduction of the debaters

• Introduction of the topic and online spot opinion poll to the audience

• 12 minutes for the first debater

• 5 minutes of rebuttal plus 12 minutes for the second debater (17 minutes in total)

• 5 minutes’ rebuttal for the first debater followed by 

• 12 minutes of Q&A and comments from participants.

• A spot online opinion poll after the Q&A to check which debater has swayed the participants more based on the initial spot opinion poll

• Vote of thanks
